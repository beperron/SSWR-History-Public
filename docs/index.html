Overview

About the Database

This database compiles metadata from all publicly available presentation abstracts at the SSWR Annual Conference from 2005 through 2026. It was constructed using a multi-stage pipeline combining web scraping from the Confex conference management system, AI-assisted extraction and classification using a small language model (gpt-oss:20b), and iterative human review.

The dataset is organized into two relational tables: papers (23,793 presentations with titles, abstracts, and methodology classifications) and paper_authors (69,924 author records with names, affiliations, positions, institutions, and geographic information). Records are linked by a shared paper identifier, enabling analyses at the presentation, author, institutional, and national levels.

The database is designed to serve as durable research infrastructure for the social work research community, supporting systematic analyses of research trends, methodological evolution, collaboration patterns, and institutional participation over time.

Growth

Conference Presentations by Year

Annual presentations grew from 423 in 2005 to 1,935 in 2026, a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.5%.

Data Quality

Quality Assurance

The database was constructed through an AI-assisted pipeline with human review at each major stage. Quality assurance included validation of web-scraped records against conference program indices, manual review of parsed affiliation fields (94% accuracy for institutions, 91% for positions in a 200-record sample), and inter-rater reliability testing for methodology classifications (Cohen’s kappa = .83).

Reporting Errors: Despite extensive quality assurance, we recognize that some errors will exist in a dataset of this scale. We welcome community contributions to improve data accuracy. If you identify an error, please submit a report through our data error reporting form.
